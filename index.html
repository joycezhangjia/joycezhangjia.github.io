About me

I'm currently a 5th-year PhD student in Routing Group, with Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Mingwei Xu. I received my B.Eng. degree in Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University in 2018. In Summer 2019, I was fortunate to visit University of Surrey and worked with Prof. Ning Wang at 5G Innovation Centre.

Research

My research interests include transport layer and learning-based network systems. In particular, I would like to improve the performance of transport layer for mobile web services.
